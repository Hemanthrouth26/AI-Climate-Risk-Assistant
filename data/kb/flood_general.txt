General Flood Safety Guidelines:
- Stay informed through local weather alerts and emergency broadcasts.
- Avoid walking, swimming, or driving through floodwaters.
- Move to higher ground immediately if flooding is imminent.
- Do not drive through flooded areas; turn around and find another route.
- Prepare an emergency kit with water, food, medications, and important documents.
- Elevate electrical appliances and valuables to prevent water damage.
- Know your community's flood evacuation routes and shelters.
- Avoid contact with floodwater, which may contain sewage or chemicals.
- After flooding, wait for official clearance before returning home.
- Document damage with photos for insurance claims.
