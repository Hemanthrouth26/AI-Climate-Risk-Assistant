General Air Quality Protection Measures:
- Stay indoors and keep windows closed during poor air quality days.
- Use air purifiers with HEPA filters in your home.
- Wear N95 or similar masks when outdoors if air quality is poor.
- Limit outdoor physical activities, especially for children and elderly.
- Avoid burning wood, trash, or other materials that contribute to pollution.
- Use public transportation or carpool to reduce vehicle emissions.
- Keep indoor air clean by ventilating when air quality improves.
- Monitor local air quality index (AQI) through apps or websites.
- Plant trees and support green initiatives to improve air quality.
- Consult healthcare providers if you have respiratory conditions.
