General Heatwave Safety Tips:
- Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water, even if you don't feel thirsty.
- Avoid strenuous outdoor activities during peak heat hours (10 AM to 4 PM).
- Wear light-colored, loose-fitting clothing and use sunscreen with high SPF.
- Check on elderly, sick, or vulnerable neighbors and family members.
- Use fans, air conditioning, or visit cooling centers if available.
- Never leave children, pets, or elderly in parked cars.
- Limit alcohol and caffeine intake, as they can dehydrate you.
- Take cool showers or baths to lower body temperature.
- Close curtains during the day to keep indoor spaces cooler.
- Prepare an emergency kit with water, non-perishable food, and medications.
